# Title: Faculty Availability

## Problem Description
Finding a faculty in their cabin during their busy schedules is a diffucult task for students.

## Proposed Solution
- Develop a notification system to notify the presence of the faculty in their cabin
- Probable availabilty timings
- Integrate time-table API to directly notify the availability
